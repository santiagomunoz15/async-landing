Martin Garrix @martingarrix

Martin Garrix is a Dutch electronic music producer and DJ, known for his infectious EDM tracks. Born on May 14, 1996, he gained fame with the hit "Animals" in 2013. His music features catchy melodies, energetic beats, and collaborations with top artists. With a humble demeanor, he has become a major influence in the global music scene.
